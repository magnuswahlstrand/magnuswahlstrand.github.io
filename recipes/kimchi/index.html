Kimchi

Ingredients

  • 1/2 napa cabbage (salladskål)
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 apple
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp paprika powder
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp sugar

How to

  1. Mix the cabbage with the salt
  2. Let sit overnight
  3. Grate the apple and mince the garlic
  4. Mix in paprika powder, chili flakes, and sugar
  5. Combine everything with the cabbage

Done!
